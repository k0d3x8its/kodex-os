# 💼 Time Log

This is an easy way for me to keep up with my work hours rather than depending on my job to do it for me. I write down when I clock in and out down to the minute. I then cross reference this with what my check stub states. My job has given me wrong hours twice in 4 years.

Each page is a work week. I use graph paper memo books for this because it makes it easier to outline the structure of each page. The page is always divided down the middle. I have one side (left) with date and times I am not supposed to be clocked out but am clocked out. On the other side (right) is my normal clock in/out time frames. It is then divided horizontally to represent 5 sections for the 5 days I work.
